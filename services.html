Our Services

At OceanBreeze, our commitment to excellence extends to a comprehensive suite of services designed to meet all your dry cargo shipbroking needs. With over two decades of industry experience, our team of dedicated professionals leverages their extensive knowledge and global network to deliver solutions that go above and beyond expectations. Explore our range of services tailored to ensure the smooth sailing of your maritime ventures:

OPERATIONS WITHIN THE DRYBULK SEGMENT

The company operates within the drybulk segment focusing in handysize up to capesize tonnage.

SOLUTIONS PROVIDER

Our team has extensive knowledge of dry cargo commodities in order optimize the timely shipment and delivery to our customers and dedicates time for better understanding of our clients enabling us to provide tailor made services.

CUSTOMER ASSISTANCE

We offer comprehensive services to our customers and the industry in the spot market as well as on short/medium/long term employment either on voyage, time charter or contracts of affreightment.

EXCLUSIVE TONNAGE MARKETING MAINTAINANCE

We maintain exclusive tonnage marketing of Greek Owners while we work with the majority of the local Owners/Managers.
